Stickies Strategic Dashboard: Features

Percentage of users that use each feature

NOTE: Cleanup and Conceal are available only to Board Creators. Data from January.

Observations

Board Creators Use Clean Up and Conceal

Out of 56 users that created a board in January, 23% used the Clean Up feature while 27% used the Conceal feature.

Sticky colors are important

Almost half (42%) of the 316 board viewers from January changed the color of a sticky.

Users highlight other user's stickies

About a third of the users click to reveal another users stickies.

Insights

Clean Up and Conceal are helpful, but...

I don't have anything to compare this to, but the fact that about one of every four board creators are using a feature suggests that they're helpful. However, this glass is only half full. I wonder why only a quarter of our board creators use these features?

Comparing the location on the page

Clean Up and Conceal are on the right side of the page while Show Other's Notes is on the left side of the page. The latter has slightly more usage, and I wonder if this is due to the location? Or maybe because users don't know what the buttons for Cleaup Up and Conceal mean?

Why do users download their data?

A large enough number of users download their data that we should ask them why. What do they do with the CSV? Is that a feature we could provide?

Recommended Actions

User interviews!

All of my insights are questions, and all of those questions could be resolved by talking with users outside of Carbon Five. Their answers will potentially guide us to make stickies more valuable, enhance usability, and increase feature usage.
